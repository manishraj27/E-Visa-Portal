Tourist Visa

A visa issued to tourists who want to visit a country for leisure or recreational purposes.

Student Visa

A visa issued to students enrolled in a course of study in a foreign country.

Work Visa

A visa issued to individuals who intend to work in a foreign country for a specific period.

Business Visa

A visa issued to individuals who want to engage in business activities in a foreign country.

Transit Visa

A visa issued to travelers passing through a country to reach their final destination.
